Graduate Transport Planner

Fore Consulting Limited | 2017 — Present

I am currently employed as a graduate transport planner at Fore Consulting. Within this role I am responsible for the production of transport assessments and travel plans and have worked on many developments of varying sizes throughout England.

I am also responsible for the development and introduction of GIS tools and processes into the companies workflows. Notably, this has included the development of a program to automate the estimation of the distribution of vehicle trips from development sites using the Google Maps API which has greatly expatiated this process, freeing up time for other work and preventing potential error associated with undertaking this process manually. I have also developed a method of automatically generating accessibility isochrones for proposed developments by processing data from Open Street Map with pgRouting and QGIS. More generally, I have introduced QGIS as a superior tool to produce the figures required for transport assessments and travel plans, resulting in more accurate and professional figures in these reports.

These accomplishments demonstrate my ability to structure and write complex reports and work efficiently to deadlines, both independently and as part of a larger team. My work introducing GIS to the company has demonstrated my ability to independently research and interpret complex concepts and to apply and pass on this knowledge to colleagues.
